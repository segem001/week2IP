Ghanian Web Application

This is an application that takes a user's birthday
and calculates the day of the week they were born
and then depending on their gender outputs their
Akan Name

Male

  • Sunday:Kwasi
  • Monday:Kwadwo
  • Tuesday:Kwabena
  • Wednesday:Kwaku
  • Thursday:Yaw
  • Friday:Kofi
  • Saturday:Kwame

Female

  • Sunday:Akosua
  • Monday:Adwoa
  • Tuesday:Abenaa
  • Wednesday:Akua
  • Thursday:Yaa
  • Friday:Afua
  • Saturday:Ama
